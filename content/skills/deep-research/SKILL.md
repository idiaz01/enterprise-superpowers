# Deep Research

Conducts thorough, multi-source investigations and delivers cited reports with proper attribution.

## When to Use

- Research requests requiring multiple sources
- Competitive analysis and market investigation
- Due diligence on technologies, companies, or approaches
- Synthesizing findings from diverse web sources

## How It Works

### Workflow

1. **Clarify goals** -- Ask 1-2 questions about intent and depth
2. **Plan research** -- Break topic into 3-5 sub-questions
3. **Multi-source search** -- Execute 15-30 unique sources using firecrawl and/or exa
4. **Deep-read sources** -- Fetch full content from 3-5 most promising URLs
5. **Synthesize report** -- Structure findings with citations, executive summary, and key takeaways
6. **Deliver** -- Post summary or save full report depending on length

### Quality Standards

- Every claim requires a source citation
- Cross-reference information across sources
- Prioritize recent sources (last 12 months)
- Flag unverified or single-source claims
- Separate facts from estimates and opinions
- Acknowledge research gaps transparently

### Search Strategy

- Use 2-3 keyword variations per sub-question
- Mix general and news-focused queries
- Prioritize academic/official sources over blogs
- For broad topics, parallelize research across multiple agents
